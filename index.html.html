About Us

At United Way Foundation, transparency is at the core of everything we do. Our team is dedicated to being open and honest in our actions as we strive to make a positive impact in our community. Here are some of our key goals and initiatives:

Our Mission

To work as a united team to achieve our goal of helping the less priveldeged.

Our Vision

We envision a society where the cycle of poverty, inequality, and suffering is broken replaced by empathy, support, and collaboration.

Our Goals

To provide food assistance to people facing hunger, with a focus on long term solutions to food, to feed 150 people for 30 days in Ramadan
